    Example architecture: Adult Social Care

    About this view

    Two approaches to visualising how a council's adult social care technology connects across LGAM layers — without implying a linear chain.

    Each shows the same information: specific systems and services involved in delivering adult social care, arranged by LGAM layer. Any element can connect to any other element in any layer.

    Option A: Zone layout

    Layers shown as spatial zones — the citizen journey flows left-to-right across the top, enabling technology sits beneath.
