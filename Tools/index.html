Architecture self-assessment

A collection of self-assessment tools to help departments and teams take an honest look at their architecture practice, agree where they want to get to, and pinpoint what to focus on next.

These tools are designed for internal use by architecture teams across government. They are anonymous, run entirely in your browser, and produce a tailored action plan with pointers to recognised government and industry standards.

How to use this collection

  1. Choose the self-assessment that best matches what you want to explore.
  2. Complete it — ideally as a team — being honest about where things really are today.
  3. Download or print the action plan, then revisit in 6 to 12 months to see how you have moved.

Available self-assessments
